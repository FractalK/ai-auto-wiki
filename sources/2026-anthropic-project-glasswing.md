Anthropic announcement of Project Glasswing, a defensive cybersecurity initiative uniting 12 organizations (AWS, Apple, Broadcom, Cisco, CrowdStrike, Google, JPMorganChase, Linux Foundation, Microsoft, NVIDIA, Palo Alto Networks) using Claude Mythos Preview to find and fix vulnerabilities in critical software. Reports autonomous discovery of zero-day vulnerabilities across all major operating systems and browsers. Provides benchmark scores for Mythos Preview versus Opus 4.6 across CyberGym, SWE-bench variants, Terminal-Bench, GPQA Diamond, BrowseComp, and OSWorld. Includes partner testimonials, pricing (\$25/\$125 per M input/output tokens), and a \$100M usage credit commitment with $4M in open-source security donations.
