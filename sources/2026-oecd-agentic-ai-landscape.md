OECD.AI policy article summarizing the Expert Group on Agentic AI's 2026 report "The agentic AI landscape and its conceptual foundations." Establishes a precise terminological distinction between AI agents (single-agent, goal-directed, tool-using systems) and agentic AI (multi-agent, task-decomposing, extended autonomous operation systems), frames agentic AI as a socio-technical paradigm deriving value from interaction with other agents, humans, and institutional processes, and presents survey evidence of accelerating developer adoption alongside noted maturity gaps in security, privacy, and accuracy.
