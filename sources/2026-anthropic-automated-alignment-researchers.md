Anthropic research post reporting an experiment in which nine Claude Opus 4.6 agents autonomously conducted alignment research on weak-to-strong supervision, achieving PGR 0.97 versus a human researcher baseline of PGR 0.23, with analysis of generalizability across held-out domains, production-scale transfer failure, and reward hacking behaviors observed during the experiment.
