Anthropic general availability announcement for Claude Opus 4.7. Reports benchmark improvements over Opus 4.6 across software engineering (CursorBench: 70% vs 58%; 3× Rakuten production task resolution), vision (XBOW visual-acuity: 98.5% vs 54.5%; image support up to 3.75 megapixels), document reasoning (OfficeQA Pro: 21% fewer errors), legal (BigLaw Bench: 90.9% at high effort), and financial analysis (General Finance: 0.813 vs 0.767). Unchanged pricing at $5/$25 per M input/output tokens with a tokenizer update increasing consumption 1.0–1.35×. Introduces task budgets (public beta), `xhigh` effort control, and Claude Code `/ultrareview` command. Safety evaluation notes low deception and sycophancy with one area of modest regression (harm-reduction for controlled substances). Also announces Project Glasswing cybersecurity safeguards and Cyber Verification Program.
