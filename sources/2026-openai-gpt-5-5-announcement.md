OpenAI's announcement of GPT-5.5 describes their flagship model as of April 2026, emphasizing agentic coding, knowledge work, and scientific research capabilities. The model achieves a claimed state-of-the-art 82.7% on Terminal-Bench 2.0 and 85.0% on ARC-AGI-2, while matching GPT-5.4's per-token serving latency and using fewer tokens for equivalent Codex tasks. OpenAI rates the model's cybersecurity and biological/chemical capabilities as "High" under its Preparedness Framework, triggering a tiered deployment model with stricter classifiers and a Trusted Access program for verified defenders. GPT-5.5 and GPT-5.5 Pro are available via ChatGPT and the API; all competitive benchmark comparisons in this announcement are vendor-sourced and should be treated with appropriate caution.
