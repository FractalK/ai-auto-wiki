Stanford CTL practice guide synthesizing pedagogical strategies for three instructor approaches to generative AI in higher education courses: Assign, Limit, and Prohibit. Covers cross-cutting policy design, process documentation, assessment alignment, and approach-specific strategies with curated further reading for each approach. Published as part of the AIMES (AI Meets Education at Stanford) resource library.
