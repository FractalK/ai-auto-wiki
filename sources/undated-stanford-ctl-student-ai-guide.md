Stanford CTL student-facing guide covering four areas: (1) checking course AI policies and Stanford's institutional policy treating substantial AI use as equivalent to receiving outside help; (2) how generative AI tools work — statistical pattern matching, hallucinations, and training data bias; (3) how AI use affects learning outcomes relative to deep cognitive engagement strategies; (4) a question framework for evaluating AI use decisions across assignment compliance, accuracy/bias, and learning impact dimensions.
