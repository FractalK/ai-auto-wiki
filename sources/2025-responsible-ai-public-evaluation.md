This report introduces and operationalizes Responsible AI for Evaluation (RAI-Ev), a five-step post hoc analytical framework for integrating AI into government program evaluation and performance auditing. Developed through research at SMU DataArts at Southern Methodist University, RAI-Ev positions AI as a tool that surfaces patterns in past program data to inform — rather than replace — human decision-making, and aligns with the five standards of the Foundations for Evidence-Based Policymaking Act of 2018. The report applies the framework to a COVID-relief arts grant program case study and provides actionable recommendations for responsible AI adoption in government evaluation contexts.
